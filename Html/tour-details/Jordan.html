6-Day Petra & Jordan Highlights

📅 Aug 18 – 23, 2026 ⏱ 6 Days / 5 Nights 👥 Group Tour 🇯🇴 Jordan

Tour Overview

Jordan is one of the Middle East's most captivating travel destinations — a country that packs an extraordinary range of experiences into a relatively small area. At its heart is Petra, the ancient Nabataean city carved directly into rose-red sandstone cliffs in the southern desert, and one of the most breathtaking archaeological sites on earth. Walking through the narrow Siq gorge and emerging to your first view of the Treasury — Al-Khazneh — is a moment that stays with travellers for life.

Beyond Petra, Jordan offers the surreal red desert landscape of Wadi Rum — a vast protected wilderness of sandstone mountains and ancient canyons where Lawrence of Arabia was filmed — plus the extraordinary experience of floating effortlessly in the hyper-saline waters of the Dead Sea, the lowest point on earth. Amman, Jordan's modern capital, rounds out the experience with its Roman ruins, vibrant food scene, and the hilltop Citadel overlooking the city.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Nairobi → Amman — Arrival & Citadel

Fly from Nairobi to Queen Alia International Airport in Amman (approx. 4–5 hours, usually via a Gulf hub). Transfer to your Amman hotel. Afternoon visit to the Amman Citadel on its hilltop position overlooking the city — home to the Temple of Hercules, the Umayyad Palace, and sweeping views over Amman's white limestone hills. Evening walk through the vibrant Rainbow Street neighbourhood, famous for its restaurants, cafés, and street food. Welcome dinner at a traditional Jordanian restaurant — mezze, mansaf, and knafeh.

Day 2
Amman — Roman Theatre, Dead Sea & Desert Castles

Morning visit to the Roman Theatre in downtown Amman — one of the best preserved Roman theatres in the Middle East, carved into the hillside and still used for performances today. Drive south to the Dead Sea — float effortlessly in its hyper-saline waters (ten times saltier than the ocean) and cover yourself in mineral-rich black mud at one of the resort beaches. Late afternoon visit to one of Jordan's Eastern Desert Castles — 8th century Umayyad hunting lodges decorated with extraordinary early Islamic frescoes and mosaics.

Day 3
Amman → Petra — The Rose Red City

Drive south to Petra (approx. 3 hours) through the stunning King's Highway — one of the world's oldest roads, passing through dramatic Jordanian highlands and ancient biblical landscapes. Arrive in Petra in the early afternoon. Enter through the Siq — a narrow 1.2km gorge with towering sandstone walls — and emerge to your first sight of Al-Khazneh, the Treasury, carved from solid rose-red rock. Explore the outer Siq and the Street of Facades before the day's light fades. Overnight in Petra town.

Day 4
Petra — Full Day Exploration

A full day inside Petra with your guide. Explore the Colonnaded Street, the Great Temple, the Royal Tombs carved into the cliff face, and the Monastery (Al-Deir) — Petra's largest monument, reached via 800 rock-cut steps with extraordinary views from the top. Optional evening Petra by Night experience — the Siq and Treasury lit by 1,500 candles after dark (additional cost, highly recommended).

Day 5
Wadi Rum Desert — Jeep Safari & Overnight Camp

Drive to Wadi Rum — the "Valley of the Moon" — a vast protected wilderness of sandstone mountains, ancient rock carvings, and sweeping red sand desert. Board a 4WD jeep for a guided safari through the desert landscape, visiting Lawrence of Arabia's spring, Khazali Canyon's ancient inscriptions, and the iconic Um Fruth rock bridge. Watch the desert sunset turn the sandstone cliffs from orange to deep crimson. Overnight in a Bedouin desert camp under one of the world's most spectacular starry skies.

Day 6
Wadi Rum → Amman → Return to Nairobi

Wake at dawn to watch the desert sunrise from your camp — one of the most serene and beautiful moments of the entire trip. After breakfast, drive back to Amman (approx. 4 hours). Brief stop at the Aqaba Red Sea coastline en route if time permits. Transfer to Queen Alia International Airport for return flight to Nairobi.
