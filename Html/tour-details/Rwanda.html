5-Day Rwanda Gorilla Trekking Adventure

📅 Jun 30 – Jul 4, 2026 ⏱ 5 Days / 4 Nights 👥 Small Group Tour 🇷🇼 Rwanda

Tour Overview

Coming face to face with a mountain gorilla in the wild is widely regarded as one of the most profound wildlife encounters on earth. Rwanda's Volcanoes National Park — a chain of mist-covered volcanic mountains in the northwest of the country — is home to roughly half of the world's remaining mountain gorilla population, and the chance to spend one hour observing a habituated gorilla family in their natural forest habitat is a once-in-a-lifetime experience that travellers consistently describe as life-changing.

Beyond the gorillas, Rwanda is a country of extraordinary beauty and resilience. Kigali, the capital, is one of Africa's cleanest and most modern cities — a testament to the country's remarkable transformation over the past three decades. This 5-day adventure combines the gorilla trek with a Kigali city tour, a visit to the moving Genocide Memorial, and time in the lush green hills of the northwest, giving you a full and honest picture of this remarkable country.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Nairobi → Kigali — Arrival & City Orientation

Fly from Nairobi to Kigali International Airport (approx. 1.5 hours). Transfer to your Kigali hotel. Afternoon orientation walk through Kigali's immaculate streets — visit the Kimironko Market, the vibrant Nyamirambo neighbourhood, and enjoy dinner at one of Kigali's excellent restaurants. Welcome briefing from your Roameo guide on the gorilla trek ahead.

Day 2
Kigali — Genocide Memorial & Cultural Tour

Morning visit to the Kigali Genocide Memorial — a powerful and moving tribute to the victims of the 1994 genocide, and an important part of understanding Rwanda's history and extraordinary recovery. Afternoon visit to the Inema Arts Centre, showcasing Rwanda's thriving contemporary art scene, followed by a craft village visit. Transfer to Musanze (Ruhengeri) in the afternoon — the gateway town to Volcanoes National Park. Dinner and overnight at lodge near the park.

Day 3
Volcanoes National Park — Mountain Gorilla Trek

The highlight of the entire trip. Wake early and transfer to the park headquarters for a 7:00am briefing with park rangers. You'll be assigned to a habituated gorilla family group and begin trekking into the forest — the hike can take anywhere from 1 to 5 hours depending on where the gorillas are that day. Once you locate the family, you'll spend one magical hour observing them at close range — silverbacks, mothers nursing infants, juveniles playing in the trees. Gorilla permits are included in your package. Return to lodge for a celebration lunch.

Day 4
Golden Monkey Trek & Twin Lakes

Morning golden monkey trekking in Volcanoes National Park — these rare and playful primates are endemic to the Virunga volcanic region and make for a wonderful wildlife experience after the gorillas. Afternoon visit to the twin lakes of Burera and Ruhondo — two stunning crater lakes in the volcanic hills with canoe rides available. Return to Kigali for final night.

Day 5
Kigali — Leisure Morning → Return to Nairobi

Final morning at leisure in Kigali — visit the Caplaki Craft Village for last-minute souvenirs, or enjoy breakfast at one of Kigali's rooftop cafés. Transfer to Kigali International Airport for return flight to Nairobi. Arrive Nairobi by early afternoon.
