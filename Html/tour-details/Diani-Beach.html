4-Day Diani Beach Getaway

📅 Aug 8 – 11, 2026 ⏱ 4 Days / 3 Nights 👥 Group Tour 🇰🇪 Kenya

Tour Overview

Diani Beach is consistently rated one of Africa's best beaches — and once you arrive, you'll understand why. Stretching for over 17 kilometres along Kenya's south coast, Diani's powder-white sand and warm turquoise Indian Ocean waters make it the perfect escape from the city. This 4-day getaway is designed for pure relaxation and coastal adventure — whether you want to snorkel above vibrant coral reefs, kiteboard across the lagoon, explore the sacred Shimba Hills, or simply lie under a palm tree with a fresh coconut in hand.

Just a 45-minute flight or a scenic 6-hour road trip south of Nairobi, Diani feels worlds away from the hustle of the capital. Our hand-picked beachfront resort puts you steps from the water, with everything you need for a seamless coastal escape.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Nairobi → Diani Beach — Arrival & Beach Afternoon

Depart Nairobi early morning by scheduled flight to Ukunda Airstrip (45 min) or by road via Mombasa (approx. 6 hours). Transfer to your beachfront resort. Check in, freshen up, and spend the afternoon at your leisure on the beach. Welcome sundowner cocktails at the beach bar as the Indian Ocean turns gold at sunset. Dinner at the resort.

Day 2
Snorkelling & Watersports Day

After breakfast, join a guided snorkelling excursion to Diani's coral reef — one of the most accessible in East Africa. Spot colourful tropical fish, sea turtles, and vibrant coral formations in the warm shallow waters. Afternoon is free for optional watersports: kiteboarding, windsurfing, jet skiing, or glass-bottom boat rides are all available directly at the beach (additional cost). Evening dinner at a local seafood restaurant in Diani town.

Day 3
Shimba Hills Day Trip & Colobus Monkey Sanctuary

Morning excursion to the Shimba Hills National Reserve — Kenya's only coastal rainforest reserve and home to the rare sable antelope, elephants, and leopards. After lunch, visit the Colobus Conservation Centre where the endangered Angolan colobus monkey is protected and rehabilitated. Return to the resort for a final beach sunset and farewell dinner.

Day 4
Leisure Morning → Return to Nairobi

Final morning at leisure — one last swim, beach walk, or breakfast on the terrace watching dhow boats drift across the lagoon. Check out and transfer to Ukunda Airstrip or Mombasa for return journey to Nairobi. Arrive Nairobi by early afternoon.
