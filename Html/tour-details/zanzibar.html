6-Day Zanzibar Beach & Culture Tour

📅 Jul 20 – 25, 2026 ⏱ 6 Days / 5 Nights 👥 Group Tour 🇹🇿 Tanzania

Tour Overview

Zanzibar — the Spice Island — is one of East Africa's most enchanting destinations. Just a short flight from Nairobi across the Indian Ocean, this Tanzanian archipelago blends a rich Swahili and Arab heritage with some of the region's most breathtaking beaches. Stone Town, Zanzibar's historic capital and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a labyrinth of narrow alleys, ornately carved wooden doors, bustling spice markets, and centuries-old mosques and palaces that tell the story of the island's layered history as a centre of the spice and slave trades.

Beyond Stone Town, the island's northern and eastern beaches — Nungwi, Kendwa, and Paje — offer crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sand, and world-class snorkelling and diving above vibrant coral reefs. This 6-day tour gives you the best of both worlds: culture and coastline.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Nairobi → Zanzibar — Arrival & Stone Town Check-in

Fly from Nairobi's Jomo Kenyatta International Airport to Zanzibar Abeid Amani Karume International Airport (approx. 2 hours). Transfer to your Stone Town boutique hotel. Afternoon at leisure to explore the winding alleys of the old town on foot. Evening welcome dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the Indian Ocean harbour.

Day 2
Stone Town Cultural Tour & Spice Farm Visit

Morning guided walking tour of Stone Town — visit the House of Wonders, the Old Fort, the former Slave Market and Memorial, and the colourful Darajani Market. Afternoon excursion to a traditional spice farm in the island's interior where you'll taste and smell cloves, vanilla, cinnamon, black pepper, and nutmeg growing fresh on the trees. Return to Stone Town for sunset at Africa House Hotel's famous terrace bar.

Day 3
Stone Town → Nungwi Beach

After breakfast, transfer to Nungwi on Zanzibar's northern tip (approx. 1 hour). Check in to your beachfront resort. Afternoon snorkelling excursion above the coral reef off Nungwi's coastline — spot sea turtles, parrotfish, and colourful reef fish in the warm, clear water. Evening dhow sunset cruise with fresh fruit and drinks.

Day 4
Nungwi Beach — Full Day at Leisure

A full free day at Nungwi Beach. Options include: scuba diving (additional cost, PADI certified instructors available), deep sea fishing, kitesurfing at Kendwa Beach, or simply relaxing under a palm tree with a fresh coconut. Visit the Mnarani Marine Turtle Conservation Pond in the afternoon to learn about the island's sea turtle protection programme.

Day 5
Paje Beach & Jozani Forest

Morning drive to Jozani Chwaka Bay National Park — home to the rare and endangered Zanzibar red colobus monkey, found nowhere else on earth. Guided forest walk with a park ranger. Afternoon at Paje Beach on the island's east coast, famous for its shallow turquoise lagoon and world-class kitesurfing conditions. Farewell seafood dinner on the beach.

Day 6
Leisure Morning → Return to Nairobi

Final morning at leisure — one last swim or a walk along the beach. Transfer to Zanzibar Airport for return flight to Nairobi. Arrive Nairobi by early afternoon.
