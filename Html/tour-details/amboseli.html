3-Day Amboseli Elephant Safari

📅 Jul 18 – 20, 2026 ⏱ 3 Days / 2 Nights 👥 Group Tour 🇰🇪 Kenya

Tour Overview

Amboseli National Park offers one of Africa's most iconic wildlife experiences — vast herds of elephants roaming freely against the dramatic backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest peak rising snow-capped across the Tanzanian border. The name "Amboseli" comes from the Maasai word meaning "salty dusty place" — and the park's open, dusty plains make for exceptional wildlife viewing, with animals visible across long distances in every direction.

Amboseli is home to one of Kenya's largest elephant populations, and the park's elephants are among the most studied in the world — many are individually known to researchers who have been tracking them for decades. On a clear morning, watching a herd of 50 elephants moving silently across the plain with Kilimanjaro's snow-capped summit glowing behind them is one of the most breathtaking sights in all of Africa.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Nairobi → Amboseli — Arrival & Afternoon Game Drive

Depart Nairobi at 7:00am by road (approx. 4 hours via Namanga highway). Arrive at your Amboseli lodge in time for lunch. Afternoon game drive across the park's open plains — watch for elephant herds, buffalo, zebra, wildebeest, and giraffe against the spectacular Kilimanjaro backdrop. Sundowner drinks as the mountain turns pink at dusk. Dinner at the lodge.

Day 2
Full Day Game Drives — Elephants & Kilimanjaro

Dawn game drive at 5:30am — the best chance for clear views of Kilimanjaro before clouds gather mid-morning. Watch elephant families moving to water, lions hunting on the open plains, and cheetah scanning the grasslands from termite mounds. Afternoon drive through the swamp areas where hundreds of elephants congregate to drink and bathe. Visit the Amboseli Elephant Research Camp observation point to learn about the park's famous elephant families.

Day 3
Final Morning Drive → Return to Nairobi

One last dawn drive before breakfast — Kilimanjaro is clearest in the early morning before heat haze builds. After breakfast, check out and depart for Nairobi. Optional visit to a Maasai cultural village en route (additional cost). Arrive Nairobi by early afternoon.
