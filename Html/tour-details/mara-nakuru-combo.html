5-Day Mara & Nakuru Combo Safari

📅 Sep 5 – 9, 2026 ⏱ 5 Days / 4 Nights 👥 Group Tour 🇰🇪 Kenya

Tour Overview

Kenya's two most iconic wildlife destinations combined into one incredible 5-day journey. Start in the legendary Maasai Mara — home to the Big Five and the world-famous wildebeest migration — then travel north to Lake Nakuru, one of the Great Rift Valley's most spectacular soda lakes. Lake Nakuru is famous for its enormous flocks of flamingos that turn the lake's shores pink, as well as its thriving populations of both black and white rhino, making it one of the best places in Kenya to see these endangered giants up close.

This combo itinerary is perfect for travellers who want to experience the diversity of Kenya's wildlife in a single trip — from open savannah big cat country to the dramatic scenery of the Rift Valley lakes.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Nairobi → Maasai Mara — Arrival & Afternoon Game Drive

Depart Nairobi at 7:00am by road. Arrive at your Mara tented camp by early afternoon. After lunch, head out for your first game drive across the open plains. Watch for lion prides, cheetah, elephant herds, and the vast wildebeest herds of the migration season. Return to camp for a campfire dinner under the stars.

Day 2
Maasai Mara — Full Day Game Drives

An entire day in the Mara ecosystem. Dawn drive at 5:30am when predators are most active — lions, leopards and cheetah are frequently spotted in the early morning light. After breakfast back at camp, afternoon drive focused on the Mara River crossing points where wildebeest brave the crocodile-filled waters. Optional hot air balloon safari at dawn (additional cost).

Day 3
Maasai Mara → Lake Nakuru

Morning game drive before breakfast, then depart for Lake Nakuru National Park (approx. 4 hours drive). Check in to your Nakuru lodge in the afternoon. Evening game drive inside the park — watch for the iconic flamingo flocks gathering along the lake's alkaline shores as the sun sets over the Rift Valley escarpment.

Day 4
Lake Nakuru — Full Day Game Drives

A full day exploring Lake Nakuru National Park. Morning drive focused on rhino tracking — both black and white rhino are regularly spotted here, making Nakuru one of Kenya's premier rhino sanctuaries. Afternoon drive along the lake shore where thousands of flamingos, pelicans, and other waterbirds gather. Look out for Rothschild giraffe, buffalo, lion, and leopard too.

Day 5
Lake Nakuru → Return to Nairobi

Final morning game drive before breakfast. Depart Lake Nakuru after breakfast and drive back to Nairobi (approx. 2.5 hours). Optional stop at Lake Naivasha for a quick boat ride or lunch at a lakeside restaurant en route. Arrive Nairobi by early afternoon.
