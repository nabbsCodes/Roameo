Common Questions

Before You Enquire

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for domestic trips, and 2–3 months for international tours. Some packages — especially gorilla trekking in Rwanda — require even earlier booking due to limited permit availability.

Do I need a visa for international tours?

Visa requirements vary by destination and passport. Our team will advise you on the specific requirements for your chosen destination and can provide guidance on the application process. Visa fees are generally not included in tour prices.

What is your deposit and payment policy?

We require a deposit of approximately 20% of the total tour price to confirm your booking. The remaining balance is due 4 weeks before departure. We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and major credit cards.

Can I customise a tour package?

Absolutely. All our tours can be customised — different dates, extended itineraries, private group departures, upgraded accommodation, or completely bespoke itineraries. Just let us know your requirements in your enquiry.
