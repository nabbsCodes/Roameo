10-Day Classic Europe Grand Tour

📅 Sep 1 – 10, 2026 ⏱ 10 Days / 9 Nights 👥 Group Tour 🌍 7 Countries

Tour Overview

Journey through seven countries in ten unforgettable days. From the romance of Paris to the canals of Amsterdam, the alpine peaks of Switzerland, and the timeless beauty of Venice and Milan — this grand tour is designed for travellers who want to experience the very best of Europe in one seamless trip. All accommodation, intercity transport, and guided city tours are included.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Paris, France — Arrival

Arrive at Charles de Gaulle Airport. Transfer to your hotel in central Paris. Evening Seine river cruise and welcome dinner.

Day 2
Paris, France — City Tour

Morning visit to the Louvre Museum, afternoon stroll along the Champs-Élysées, and an evening visit to the Eiffel Tower.

Day 3
Brussels, Belgium

Travel to Brussels by train. Visit the Grand Place, enjoy Belgian chocolate tasting, and explore the EU Quarter.

Day 4
Amsterdam, Netherlands

Morning canal cruise, afternoon visit to the Anne Frank House and Van Gogh Museum. Evening free time in the Jordaan district.

Day 5
Cologne, Germany

Travel to Cologne. Visit the iconic Cologne Cathedral and take a scenic walk along the Rhine riverside.

Day 6
Lucerne, Switzerland

Arrive in Lucerne. Visit the Chapel Bridge, stroll the old town, and take the Mount Pilatus cable car for panoramic alpine views.

Day 7
Vaduz, Liechtenstein & Innsbruck, Austria

Brief stop in Vaduz for photos of the castle, then continue to Innsbruck. Evening walk through the old town and Golden Roof.

Day 8
Vienna, Austria

Visit the magnificent Schönbrunn Palace, St. Stephen's Cathedral, and the famous Viennese coffee houses.

Day 9
Venice, Italy

Arrive in Venice. Gondola ride through the Grand Canal, visit St. Mark's Square and the Doge's Palace.

Day 10
Milan, Italy — Departure

Morning visit to Milan Cathedral and the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II. Transfer to Milan Malpensa Airport for departure.
