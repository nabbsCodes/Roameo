How It All Started

From One Safari to a World of Adventures

Roameo was founded in 2018 by a group of Nairobi-based travel enthusiasts who believed that Kenyans deserved access to world-class travel experiences — both at home and abroad. What started as a small operation running weekend safaris to the Maasai Mara quickly grew into a full-service travel company connecting travellers from Kenya to destinations across Africa, the Middle East, and Europe.

Today, Roameo operates over 10 curated tour packages, has served more than 500 happy travellers, and maintains trusted partnerships with hotels, airlines, and ground operators across 18 destinations worldwide. Our mission remains simple: take the stress out of travel and replace it with wonder.
